Reasons why i love to sing the sunshine song

  • The moon interchanges with it
  • The stars are also close to the sunshine
  • Sunshine is affliated with a new day which signifies new things

Top 3 reasons i love to sing the sunshine to my baby girl

  1. It makes her sleep immediately I am done
  2. Keeps her happy despite that it makes her cry
  3. Gets me to remember how beautiful the sun is and how it shines
